# Gigster.co.za Cost Guidelines (South Africa)
Source: https://www.gigster.co.za/cost-guidelines/
Note: Prices exclude VAT, transport, accommodation, meals, per diems, travel time, technical equipment, rehearsals, waiting time, and any other extras.

## BANDS
- Celebrity / Big Name / Headliner Bands: ± R50,000 upwards for 1 × 60-90 min set
- Corporate Function / Show Bands: ± R20,000 - R60,000 for 2-3 × 45 min sets
- Private Function Bands: R8,000 - R30,000 for 3-4 × 45 min sets
- Small to medium Ethnic / African Bands (e.g. marimba): ± R6,000 - R12,000 for 2-3 hours
- Larger African Bands: ± R25,000 upwards for 2-3 × 45 min sets

## MUSICIANS
- Professional Solo Instrumentalists (saxophone, violin etc.): ± R3,000 upwards
- Vocalist (with backing tracks): from ± R8,000 upwards
- One-man Band: ± R6,000 - R15,000
- Classical Duo: ± R5,000 upwards
- Classical Trio: ± R7,000 upwards
- Classical Quartet: ± R9,000 upwards
- African Choir: ± R8,000 - R15,000
- Function DJs: ± R7,000 - R15,000

## COMEDIANS
- Professional (non-celeb): ± R12,000 - R20,000 for 30-40 min comedy show
- Famous / Celebrity: ± R25,000 - R50,000 for 30-40 min comedy show
- Famous / Celebrity comedy MC: ± R25,000 - R55,000 for 4 hours, including short comedy set

## PRESENTERS / MCs
- Professional (non-celeb): ± R15,000 - R20,000 for up to 4 hours
- Famous / Celebrity: ± R25,000 - R55,000 for up to 4 hours

## SPEAKERS
- Professional (non-celeb): ± R12,000 - R20,000
- Famous / Celebrity: ± R25,000 - R45,000

## MAGICIANS
- Professional Close-up/Roaming Magician: ± R5,000 - R10,000 for 1-2 hours
- Professional Stage Magic Show: ± R10,000 - R20,000 for 1 × 30-40 min show
- Professional Illusion Show: ± R25,000 - R45,000 for 1 × 30-40 min show
